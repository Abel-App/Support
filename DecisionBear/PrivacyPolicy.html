Decision Bear - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025.9.15

This Privacy Policy explains how Decision Bear ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information from users of our services. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and non-personal information to provide and improve our services.

1.1 Device and Technical Information

We may automatically collect technical information about your device, including but not limited to:

  • IP address, country code, time zone
  • Device identifiers (IDFV), language, network status
  • Device model, operating system, CPU, storage, screen size, firmware
  • SDK version, application API keys, push notification token
  • Browser type and version, browser plugins

Some of this information may uniquely identify your device, but it does not personally identify you in most cases. Certain laws (e.g., in the U.S.) may consider device identifiers as personal information.

1.2 Personal Information

When using our services, you may provide personally identifiable information ("Personal Data"), including but not limited to:

  • Mobile phone number
  • Email address
  • Address details

This information is collected only to provide services and improve user experience.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for purposes including, but not limited to:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving services
  • Notifying users about service changes
  • Enabling interactive features
  • Providing customer support
  • Analyzing and monitoring service usage
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues

When you access our services via mobile devices, we may collect device type, device ID, IP address, mobile operating system, browser type, and other diagnostic data.

3. Information Storage and Security

3.1 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information. However, no security system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

3.2 Data Transfer

Your information may be transferred to servers outside your country, where data protection laws may differ. By providing your information, you consent to such transfers.

3.3 Data Disclosure

We may disclose your information if necessary:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To prevent or investigate potential misconduct related to services

3.4 Service Providers

We may use third-party service providers to perform functions on our behalf. They are only allowed to access your information as necessary for their services and must comply with this Privacy Policy.

3.5 Links to Other Websites

Our services may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

4. Data Deletion

When information is no longer necessary, we take reasonable measures to delete it. We may also remove user-generated content that violates our policies (e.g., pornographic or violent content). Users can report inappropriate content.

5. Protection of Minors

We value the privacy of minors.

  • Under 14: Users must obtain parental or guardian consent.
  • Between 14 and 18: Users should ensure they or their guardians consent.
  • If we learn personal data of children under 14 has been collected without consent, we will delete it as soon as possible.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve our services:

  • Session Cookies: Required for service functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Security Cookies: Ensure security of the services

You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.
